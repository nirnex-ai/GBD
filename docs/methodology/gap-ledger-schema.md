# Gap Ledger Schema

## Canonical Columns

| Area | Gap Item | Status | Reason | Remarks (Code-Grounded Assessment) | Priority | Depends On | Dependency | Execution Order |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Recommended Controlled Values

### Status

- Not Developed
- Stubbed
- Partial
- Implemented-Unverified
- Verified
- Blocked

### Priority

- High
- Medium
- Low

### Depends On

- Yes
- No

### Dependency

Use explicit gap IDs or names.

### Execution Order

Use integer or sequence labels.

## Strong Recommendation

Add internal fields even if not displayed in all views:

- Gap ID
- Proof Required

Without stable IDs, dependency management becomes fragile.
